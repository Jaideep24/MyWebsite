ADLABS IMAGICA

Adlabs Imagica is the largest theme park of the country situated in Mumbai. Inaugurated in 2013 and sprawling over a humongous area of 300 acres, the Adlabs Imagica is a one stop destination for all water cum adventure activities. Besides, it also boasts of a plethora of restaurants and club both for luxury dining and binge eating.Adlabs Imagica has every ride you could think of, from kids to adults to the oldies, there something for people of every age group, one could never get bored here. And thats why this amusement park stands first in our list of top five amusement parks.
