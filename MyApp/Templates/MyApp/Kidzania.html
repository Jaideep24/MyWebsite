KIDZANIA

After the biggest one comes the best indoor. Kidzania is a themed indoor amusement destination which is in itself a mini world. This place is the one stop destination for curios kids who want to explore diefferent careers. From pilot to fire fighter, from a salesman to doctor, the possibilities are endless. It also gives kids a sense of basic economics thanks to their very own currency called kidzos, do fun-filled jobs to earn kidzos and then spend them at other places. Kids get to have their own credit cards and driving licenses after a driving test. They even have a lounge for the parents so that they can relax while their kids go crazy in this amazing place. So if you and your little ones wanna take a break from the daily hustle bustle, bring them to RCity Mall, Ghatkopar.
